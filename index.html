The Automatic Timetable Generator is a smart, user-friendly platform designed to simplify scheduling for schools and colleges. It automates class and teacher assignments, saving time and reducing manual work. Administrators can create timetables effortlessly, teachers can view and manage their schedules, and students get instant access to their classes. With real-time updates and easy adjustments, it ensures smooth, hassle-free timetable management for everyone.
